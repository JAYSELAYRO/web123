Chef JP ANGLO

Head Chef

Chef JP Anglo has gained recognition for his expertise in blending traditional Filipino flavors with contemporary techniques. His dedication to showcasing Filipino cuisine earned him the title of Champion for Philippine Cuisine by Tatler Dining in 2020.

Chef Jordan Andino

Sous Chef

With over 15 years of experience, Filipino-Canadian chef Jordan Andino developed a passion for Filipino cuisine in his family's kitchen. Known for his innovative approach to Filipino dishes, Chef Jordan brings fresh creativity to every plate.

Chef Mikey Bustos

Food Critic Specialist & Culinary Innovator

With over a decade in culinary arts and food critique, Chef Mikey Bustos ensures only the finest, authentic Filipino flavors reach the table, blending heritage with modern innovation.
